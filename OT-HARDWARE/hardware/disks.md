## Discos

| Protocolos | Interfaz | Formatos |
| ---   | ---   | ---   |
| SATA  | SATA  | (HDD 3.5" 2n.5") (SSD 2.5") (SSD M.2) |
| NVMe  | PCIe  | (SSD M.2) |

- `HDD SATA`: 200–280 MB/s
- `SDD SATA 2.5"`: 530–560 MB/s
- `SDD M.2 SATA"`: 530–560 MB/s
- `SSD M.2 PCIe 3.0 x 4`: 3000–3500 MB/s
- `SSD M.2 PCIe 4.0 x 4`: 6800–7500 MB/s
- `SSD M.2 PCIe 5.0 x 4`: 12000–14500 MB/s

## Conexiones Externas:

- `USB 3.0`: ~450 MB/s
- `USB 3.2 Gen2`: ~1000 MB/s
- `USB 3.2 Gen2x2`: ~2000 MB/s
- `Thunderbolt3/4`: ~2800–3000 MB/s
